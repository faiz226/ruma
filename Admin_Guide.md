# RUMA Homestay - Admin Booking Guide 🏠
*Panduan Pengurusan Tempahan RUMA Homestay*

Welcome to your RUMA Admin Dashboard! This guide will show you how to manage your bookings manually after confirming them with your guests via WhatsApp.

Selamat datang ke Admin Dashboard RUMA! Panduan ini akan menunjukkan cara menguruskan tempahan secara manual selepas anda mengesahkannya dengan tetamu melalui WhatsApp.

---

## 1. How to Log In (Cara Log Masuk)
1. Go to your website and add `/admin` to the end of the URL.
   *(Pergi ke laman web anda dan tambah `/admin` di hujung URL.)*
2. Enter your Admin Email and Password. 
   *(Masukkan Emel dan Kata Laluan Admin anda.)*
3. Click **Sign In**.
   *(Klik **Sign In**.)*

---

## 2. How to Create a Booking (Cara Memasukkan Tempahan Baru)
When a guest confirms their dates and pays you via WhatsApp/Bank Transfer:
*(Apabila tetamu mengesahkan tarikh dan membayar melalui WhatsApp/Bank Transfer:)*

1. Look for the blue **+ Create Booking** button at the top right of your dashboard.
   *(Cari butang biru **+ Create Booking** di bahagian atas kanan dashboard anda.)*
2. A pop-up form will appear. Fill in the guest's details:
   *(Satu borang akan muncul. Isikan maklumat tetamu:)*
   - **Guest Name (Nama Tetamu)**
   - **Email (Emel Tetamu)** *(Required so they can receive the confirmation!)*
   - **Phone (No. Telefon)**
   - **Check-in & Check-out Dates (Tarikh Masuk & Keluar)**
3. Click **Confirm & Send Automations**.
   *(Klik **Confirm & Send Automations**.)*

**✨ What happens magically next? (Apa yang berlaku secara automatik?)**
- The dates are immediately blocked on your website calendar. *(Tarikh ditutup serta-merta di laman web.)*
- A professional email receipt is sent to the guest. *(Resit emel rasmi dihantar kepada tetamu.)*
- The dates are blocked on your Google Calendar. *(Tarikh ditutup di Google Calendar anda.)*
- The booking is logged in your Google Sheets for accounting. *(Rekod disimpan dalam Google Sheets.)*

---

## 3. How to Modify a Booking (Cara Menukar Tarikh Tempahan)
If a guest wants to change their check-in/check-out dates:
*(Jika tetamu ingin menukar tarikh masuk/keluar mereka:)*

1. Find their booking in your list.
   *(Cari tempahan mereka dalam senarai.)*
2. Click the **Modify** button next to their name.
   *(Klik butang **Modify** bersebelahan nama mereka.)*
3. Select the new Check-in and Check-out dates.
   *(Pilih tarikh masuk dan keluar yang baru.)*
4. Click **Save Changes**. 
   *(Klik **Save Changes**.)*

*Note: The system will automatically recalculate the price, update Google Calendar, and email the guest their new dates!*
*(Nota: Sistem akan mengira semula harga baru, kemaskini Google Calendar, dan menghantar emel kepada tetamu secara automatik!)*

---

## 4. How to Cancel a Booking (Cara Membatalkan Tempahan)
If a guest cancels or you need to free up the dates:
*(Jika tetamu batal atau anda perlu membuka semula tarikh tersebut:)*

1. Find their booking in your list.
   *(Cari tempahan mereka dalam senarai.)*
2. Click the red **Cancel** button.
   *(Klik butang merah **Cancel**.)*
3. Click **OK** to confirm.
   *(Klik **OK** untuk mengesahkan.)*

*Note: The system will automatically open the dates back up on your website, delete the event from Google Calendar, and send a cancellation email to the guest.*
*(Nota: Sistem akan membuka semula tarikh di laman web, memadam acara di Google Calendar, dan menghantar emel pembatalan kepada tetamu.)*
